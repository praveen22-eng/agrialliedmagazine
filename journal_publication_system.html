About the Journal

Our Mission

The Journal of Food & Agricultural Systems is dedicated to advancing knowledge and practice in sustainable agriculture and food systems through peer-reviewed research, case studies, and practical insights.

We believe in the power of open access to democratize agricultural knowledge and empower farmers, researchers, and policymakers worldwide.

  • Peer-reviewed publications
  • Open access to all articles
  • Rapid publication process
  • Global author and reviewer network
  • Focus on sustainable practices

Author Guidelines

Submission Requirements

  • Manuscript Length: 3,000 - 8,000 words
  • Format: PDF or Word (.doc, .docx)
  • References: Minimum 15 scholarly sources
  • Citation Style: APA 7th Edition
  • Language: English (international English accepted)
  • Figures/Tables: High resolution (300 DPI minimum)

Peer Review Process

  • Review Type: Double-blind peer review
  • Reviewers: Typically 2-3 independent experts
  • Timeline: 6-12 weeks
  • Possible Outcomes: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject
  • Publication: Within 2 weeks of final acceptance
  • DOI: Each article receives unique digital identifier
